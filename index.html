Family Tree Keeper

Family tree & special days

Family Tree Keeper helps you bring your whole family together in one private, beautifully organized app. Add your relatives, build an interactive family tree, and never miss a birthday, anniversary, or special day again — all stored safely on your own device.

  • Build an interactive, zoomable family tree
  • Automatic reminders for birthdays and anniversaries
  • 100% private — your data stays on your device
  • Available in English and Turkish
