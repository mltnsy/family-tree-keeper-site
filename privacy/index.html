Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

This privacy policy explains how Family Tree Keeper handles your personal data.

Data We Collect

The app stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • First name, last name, and relationship
  • National ID number
  • Birth date, profession, and wedding anniversary
  • Photos selected from your device
  • Phone number and email (optional)
  • Personal notes

Data Storage

All family data you enter is stored locally on your device only. It is never uploaded to our servers or any cloud service. Deleting the app permanently removes this data from your device.

Advertising & Analytics

Family Tree Keeper is free to use and shows ads served by Google AdMob. To request permission before using device identifiers for personalized ads, the app uses Apple's App Tracking Transparency and Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) consent flow, as required in the EEA, UK, and elsewhere. You can review or change your ad consent choice at any time from Settings → Ad Consent.

Depending on your consent choice, AdMob and its advertising partners may process device identifiers and other technical data (such as approximate location derived from your IP address) to select and measure ads. This data is handled by Google and its partners under their own privacy policies, not by us — we do not receive or store it.

Purchases

Removing ads is available as a one-time in-app purchase. Purchases are processed by Apple and validated through RevenueCat. We do not see or store your payment details.

App Permissions

The app may request the following device permissions:

  • Camera — to take photos for family members
  • Photo Library — to choose existing photos
  • Notifications — to remind you of birthdays and special days

Data Security

Your locally stored data is protected by your device's operating system security layers.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us on GitHub.
